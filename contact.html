Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and accreditation process.

How long does the accreditation process take?

The accreditation process typically takes between 3-6 months, depending on the complexity of your application and the responsiveness of all parties involved. With our expert guidance, we can help streamline this process and ensure all requirements are met efficiently.

Do you provide support after accreditation?

Yes, we offer ongoing support to ensure your continued compliance with QCTO standards. This includes regular updates to learning materials, assistance with moderation processes, and consultation on any changes to accreditation requirements.

Can I purchase individual training manuals?

Absolutely! While we offer comprehensive packages, all our training manuals are available for individual purchase. This allows you to select exactly what you need for your specific training requirements.

Do you offer customized training solutions?

Yes, we specialize in developing customized training solutions tailored to your organization's specific needs. Our team will work closely with you to understand your requirements and develop materials that align with your goals and industry standards.
