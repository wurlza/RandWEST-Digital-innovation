Our Story

Our Journey Begins

Founded in 2025, RandWest Digital Skills Academy emerged from a recognized need for high-quality, accessible digital skills training in South Africa. Our founders, experienced in both education and industry, saw the gap between traditional learning methods and the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

We started with a simple yet powerful vision: to create learning materials that don't just meet accreditation standards, but exceed them. Materials that are practical, engaging, and truly prepare individuals and organizations for the digital economy.

Today, we're proud to be a leading provider of QCTO-aligned training resources, helping hundreds of training providers achieve accreditation and thousands of learners develop essential digital skills.
